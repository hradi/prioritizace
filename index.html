A complete overview of school assignments listed in four categories. Urgent tasks include everything that is needed to be done within a few days or requires a lot of effort and time. Long-term tasks contain all assignments with a due date later than a week and consistent effort is called for. Not serious consists of all minor or mundane assignments of low importance or difficulty; these don't need extra attention. Finally, tasks in progress are stored in an individual list for better organization.
